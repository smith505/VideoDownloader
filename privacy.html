Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Universal Video Downloader. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our video downloading service.

Information We Collect

We are proud to say that we collect minimal information:

  • No Personal Information: We do not require registration, email addresses, or any personal details.
  • No Download History: We do not store or track the videos you download.
  • Temporary Processing Data: Video URLs are processed in real-time and immediately discarded after download completion.
  • Server Logs: Basic server logs may temporarily record IP addresses and timestamps for security and debugging purposes only. These are automatically deleted after 7 days.

How We Use Your Information

The minimal data we process is used solely to:

  • Process your video download requests
  • Maintain and improve service performance
  • Prevent abuse and ensure security
  • Debug technical issues

Third-Party Services

Our service may interact with third-party platforms:

  • Google AdSense: If you choose to watch ads to support us, Google AdSense may use cookies. Please review Google's Privacy Policy.

  • Video Platforms: We fetch videos from various platforms (YouTube, TikTok, etc.) on your behalf. These platforms may have their own tracking.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies for analytics or advertising purposes. The only cookies used are:

  • Session cookies necessary for the service to function
  • Third-party cookies from Google AdSense (if you choose to view ads)

Data Storage and Security

  • Downloaded files are temporarily stored on our server during the download process and immediately deleted after transfer
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect our infrastructure
  • All connections are encrypted using HTTPS
  • We do not maintain any database of user activities or download history

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's minimal information to manage. However, you have the right to:
